SOCIS Annual General Meeting Election 2014

March 5, 2014

SOCIS is pleased to announce the new executive team for the 2014-2015 school year. It was an exciting election with one of the largest turnouts we've had in years. Your new SOCIS executives are:

  • President: Ryan Wilson-Perkin
  • Vice President: Taylor Robinson
  • Secretary: Sarah Shepherd
  • Treasurer: Mia Kilborn
  • Community Liaison: Julian Horvat
  • System Administrator: Alex Pennells
  • Assistant System Administrator: Nick Bern

Thanks to everyone who came out last night!
