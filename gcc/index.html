Guelph Coding Community

The Guelph Coding Community has been organizing student run Tech Talks since 2013. GCC talks are currently being held every other Thursday night at 5:30pm in the THRN 2420 Mac Lab. Check the site for upcoming sessions and information about the speakers and their topics.

If you would like to give your own talk during a GCC event, feel free to email the GCC organizers!

There is a sign-up page for the Winter 2018 sessions located here.
