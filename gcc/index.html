Guelph Coding Community

The Guelph Coding Community has been organizing student run Tech Talks since 2013. They are typically held every other Wednesday night at 7:00pm in the Reynold's Mac Lab during the Fall and Winter semesters. Check the site for upcoming sessions and information about the speakers and their topics.

If you would like to give your own talk during a GCC event, feel free to email the SOCIS Exec team. We would love to hear about your area of interest.
