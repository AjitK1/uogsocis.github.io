SOCIS

Student organization for the University of Guelph's School of Computer Science. We run community-building events from hackathons to LANs. We also run one of the largest College Royale events, Roboticon. Come join us in the SOCIS lounge, Reynolds 104, anytime.

Front of Reynolds, by Jason Swaby (by-nc-nd)

Computer Science

We are a group of computer scientist students for computer scientist students. We coordinate with the School of Computer Science in planning the future of Computer Science at Guelph. We also manage a number of services for students like the CS lounge which is a space for relaxation and socializing.

Community Building

We run events throughout the year to build the community in the Computer Science department at Guelph. In the past we have run Hackathons and LANs and Paintballing, however we are always looking for new events to run so are always looking for suggestions from our fellow students

Roboticon

Roboticon is an anual effort by the School of Computer Science and SOCIS to foster high schoolers’ interest in the field of Computer Science. Competitors are tasked with building Lego Mindstorms robots to complete a series of challenges designed by SOCIS members.
